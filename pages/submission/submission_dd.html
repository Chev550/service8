Design Development Submission Instruction

Design Development submission is due on 18 Nov 2016. You may submit your documents via either 百度网盘 or Dropbox. Please refer to the following detailed instruction.

百度网盘

  1. 登陆百度网盘（https://pan.baidu.com），新建文件夹并命名为Team XXX Design Development Submission（将XXX替换为赛队名称）。
  2. 将所有Design Development文件上传至所新建的文件夹中。
  3. 将该文件夹分享给SDC组委会。
  4. 创建私密链接。
  5. 将链接地址和提取密码发送至support@sdchina.org.cn（也可通过邮箱发送）。
    6. Team:

Dropbox

  1. Log in to your Dropbox account (https://www.dropbox.com). Creat a new folder named Team XXX Design Development Submission (replace XXX with your team name).
  2. Upload all your Design Development documents into the folder you just created.
  3. Share your folder with the SDC Organizing Committee.
  4. Input email address support@sdchina.org.cn and optional message. Click the Share button.
  5. Done!
